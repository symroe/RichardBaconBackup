   Richard Bacon MP welcomes new Diss hostel 
  

South Norfolk MP Richard Bacon has officially opened a new hostel for homeless single men in Diss. 

Mr Bacon said: “Solo Housing is a thoroughly impressive organisation that provides hope to young men who are desperately trying to find an alternative to shuttling between prison and a life blighted by crime or addiction.

“So many people have worked incredibly hard to make this hostel happen.  The hostel is good news for Diss and I wish Solo Housing every success for the future”.

26 November 2009
