  ‘Buy Local this Christmas’ says MP

 

LOCAL MP Richard Bacon is encouraging South Norfolk residents to support their local shops in the run up to Christmas and beyond.

Mr Bacon said: “Small independent shops are really feeling the pinch in the current economic downturn. If everyone chooses to shop locally it really will make a difference.
 

  
 

Richard Bacon MP buys his Christmas card
stamps at Harleston Post Office from
postmaster Howard Farmer (left).

 
 

“Harleston and Diss have a wide variety of excellent shops offering a huge array of everyday items as well as gifts for family and friends. Both market towns have a lot going for them and it is important to see them thriving”.

Mr Bacon visited Harleston Post Office to buy his Christmas Card stamps from postmaster Howard Farmer.
