MP goes online for EDP debate

SOUTH NORFOLK MP Richard Bacon has answered questions posed by readers of the Eastern Daily Press, as part of the EDP's 'On the Spot' feature. The EDP's readers and website users got the chance to put their questions and opinions to Mr Bacon in a live online debate.

The debate saw Mr Bacon tackling areas as diverse as the re-packaged European Constitution, Norwich's bid for unitary status and sustainable housing.

Mr Bacon said today: “I was delighted to be able to answer readers' questions, which covered local, national and international issues.  The EDP’s ‘On the Spot’ series is a great idea and I thank the Eastern Daily Press for inviting me to participate”.

3 July 2007
