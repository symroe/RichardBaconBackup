  MP celebrates opening of
hi-tech classrooms
 

 
Richard is shown around the new facilities by the
pupils of Wymondham High School and new
head teacher, David Brunton (second left)

Click on the picture to see a larger version

LOCAL MP Richard Bacon joined with new head teacher Mr David Brunton, staff, students and local dignitaries to celebrate the opening of the new hi-tech classroom block at Wymondham High School.

The new block, which was opened by the school's former head teacher Mr David Walker,  is   fitted  with

 
 

the very latest education technology. Teachers will have the ability to upload their lessons to interactive whiteboards from across the school.

Mr Bacon said: “I was very impressed by the technology that will now be available to Wymondham High School's staff and students. I.T. is of increasing importance in the modern workplace and this kind of investment will definitely give the school's students the edge when they enter the job market”.
 

“The staff, pupils, governors and parents should be justifiably proud of what they have achieved”.

Wymondham High School has specialist school status in the performing arts and received a 'very good' rating from OFSTED at its last inspection.
