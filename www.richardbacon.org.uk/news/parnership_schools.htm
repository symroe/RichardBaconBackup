  MP celebrates the launch of partnership schools
 

 
Richard Bacon MP (right), with
Chair of Governors Keith Ambrose,
Headteacher Sue Cooke and pupils,
unveils a commemorative plaque at
Burston School at the launch of its
partnership with Tivetshall Primary
School

SOUTH NORFOLK MP Richard Bacon helped Burston and Tivetshall Primary Schools to celebrate the launch of their partnership by unveiling matching commemorative plaques first at Burston Community Primary School and later at Tivetshall Primary School.

Headteacher of Burston School Sue Cooke will take on the role of head of both schools, dividing her time equally between the two.

  
 

Mr Bacon said: “This is an exciting time for the schools. I am sure that both Burston and Tivetshall schools will benefit greatly by combining resources and ideas. Mrs Cooke runs an excellent school at Burston and I know she will bring her many skills to bear at Tivetshall. I wish her and both schools every success for the future”.
