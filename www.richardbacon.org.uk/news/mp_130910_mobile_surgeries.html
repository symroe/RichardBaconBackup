MP announces dates and venues for open advice surgeries


South Norfolk MP Richard Bacon has announced dates of his forthcoming ‘mobile’ advice surgeries to be held in supermarkets, community centres and village halls across the constituency during September:

TUESDAY 17 SEPTEMBER 2013:

10:00am In the café at the Sainsbury’s store on the Longwater retail park, Costessey, NR5 0JS.
