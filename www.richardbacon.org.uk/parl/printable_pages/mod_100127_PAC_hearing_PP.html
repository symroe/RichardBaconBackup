MP quizzes top MoD mandarin on severity of budget shortfall

South Norfolk MP Richard Bacon has quizzed top Ministry of Defence officials on their decision to slow down the construction of the new Queen Elizabeth Class Aircraft Carriers at a cost to taxpayers of an additional £674 million.

Questioning Sir Bill, Mr Bacon said: “I am glad that the NAO has published this figure; it is reality which the MoD has been hiding from for a long time, is it not? Ducking and weaving and if there is a problem with an Equipment Programme that is supposed to take eight years you suddenly say, ‘We will just have it take nine years’ as if somehow time is a free given. It is not”.
