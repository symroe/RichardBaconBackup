Richard on NHS I.T.

Information Technology in the NHS: What Next?
In a paper on the future of the controversial NHS computer system, MPs Richard Bacon and Dr John Pugh ask the NHS to rethink plans to upgrade its computer network and prevent the programme ‘sleepwalking to disaster’ and wasting billions of pounds of taxpayers' money.

‘Nothing to show’ for £2.7bn spend on electronic patient records - MP

Richard discusses this report on BBC Breakfast - 3 August 2011 

SOUTH NORFOLK MP Richard Bacon, a member of the Commons public accounts committee, has said that the Department of Health is unable to show what it has achieved for the £2.7 billion spent so far on detailed care records systems.
